Alejandra's SeaFood Linguine Recipe

Prep Time: 10
Cook Time: 15 mins
Total time: 25 mins
Servings: 4-6

Ingredients

  • 1lb linguine
  • 1lb shrimp, peeled and deveined
  • 1/2lb bay scallop
  • 2 tablespoons extra virgin olive oil
  • 3 tablespoons garlic, minced
  • 3/4 teaspoon crushed red pepper flakes
  • 3 tablespoons butter
  • 1 cup white wine(sauvignon blanc)
  • 1 teaspoon lemon zest
  • sea salt
  • ground black pepper, to taste
  • fresh parsley, chopped, to garnish

Instructions

  1. Cook linguini according to package directions.
  2. In large skillet over medium heat, warm the olive oil.
  3. Add garlic and crushed red pepper and cook for a couple of minutes until fragant.
  4. Melt a couple tablespoons of the butter into the olive oil and garlic, and add the shrimp.
  5. Add the white wine and bring to a boil.
  6. Reduce heat and simmer for another 3-5 minutes until wine reduces.
  7. Add scallops, remaining 1 tbsp of butter and lemon zest.
  8. Cook sea scallops for another couple minutes until warmed through.
  9. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  10. When pasta is cooked, drain and add to large serving bowl.
  11. Top linguini with the seafood and white wine and sprinkle with the chopped parsley.
  12. Let pasta sit for a couple of minutes covered in foil to absorb some of the sauce before serving.
