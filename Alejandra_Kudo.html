Alejandra's Creme Brulee Recipe

Prep Time: 15 min
Cook Time: 1 hr
Inactive Time: 2hr 15 min
Total Time: 3 hr 30 min
Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1qrt heavy cream
  • 1 vanilla bean, split scraped
  • 1 cup vanilla sugar, divided
  • 6 large egg yolks
  • 2qrt hot water

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven at 325 degrees F.
  2. Place the cream, vanilla bean and its pulp into a medium saucepan set over medium-high heat and bring to a boal. Remove from the heat, cover and allow to sit for 15 minutes. Remove vanilla bean and reserve for another use.
  3. In medium bowl, whisk together 1/2 cup sugar and the egg yolks until well blended and it just starts to lighten in color.
  4. Add the cream a little at a time, stirrings continually. Pour the liquid into 6 (7 to 8 ounce) ramekins. Place ramekins into a large cake pan or roasting pan. Pour enough hot water into the pan to come halfway up the sides of the ramekins.
  5. Bake just until the creme brulee is set, but still trembling in the center, approximately 40-45 minutes.
  6. Remove the ramekins from the roasting pan and refrigerate for at least 2 hours and up to 3 days.
  7. Remove the creme brulee from the fridge for at least 30 minutes prior to browning the sugar on top.
  8. Divide the remaining 1/2 cup vanilla sugar equally among the 6 dishes and spread evenly on top.
  9. Using a torch, melt the sugar and form a crispy top. Allow the creme brulee to sit for at least 5 minutes before serving
